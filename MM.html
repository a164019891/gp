Multi-view Gait Recognition

    As one of biometric identification methods, gait recognition has many advantages. It is suitable for human identification at a long distance, requiring no contact and hard to imitate. One of the most important factors that affect the performance of gait recognition is that the change of view or the direction of the walk, which makes the human silhouette have a large variation.
