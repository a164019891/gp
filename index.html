YaZhou Yao（姚亚洲）, Ph.D.
Global Big Data Technologies Center, University of Technology Sydney, Australia
  •  Yazhou.Yao@outlook.com

I am currently a Professor at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology . I obtained my Ph.D. degree in June 2018 from Global Big Data Technologies Center, University of Technology Sydney , Australia (advised by Prof. Jian Zhang). From July 2018 to July 2019, I worked as a Research Scientist at Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Abu Dhabi, UAE (led by Prof. Ling Shao). From 2013 to 2014, I studied at the School of computer science and engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Zhenmin Tang. I obtained my Bachelor and Master degree in 2010 and 2013 from Nanjing Normal University .

Research interests: Multimedia, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning.
